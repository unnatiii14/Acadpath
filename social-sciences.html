Social Sciences

Overview

The Social Sciences program at AcadPath offers a deep dive into understanding human behavior, social dynamics, and global political systems. Our courses are grounded in empirical research and aim to create thought leaders who drive social change.

Programs Offered

  • Sociology - Explore the structure and dynamics of societies.
  • Psychology - Understand the human mind and behavior.
  • Political Science - Study the intricacies of political systems and international relations.
  • Anthropology - Examine cultural diversity and human evolution.

Career Opportunities

Graduates from our Social Sciences programs excel in:

  • Policy Analysis
  • Social Work
  • Human Resources
  • Market Research
  • Nonprofit Management

Our alumni work with NGOs, government agencies, and international organizations such as the UN and WHO.
